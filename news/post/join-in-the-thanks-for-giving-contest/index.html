Join in the "Thanks-For-Giving" Contest!

Posted by: The Toontown Relived Team on November 27, 2019 at 3:57 PM

Thanksgiving is tomorrow and what better way to celebrate other than giving to others! Give thanks to your friends and local shopkeepers by entering the "Thanks-For-Giving" contest!

From now until December 7th, you can participate in the "Thanks-For-Giving" contest! All you have to do is place in the top three in two categories: Most Gifts Given, and Most Cog Buildings Defeated. Give thanks to your friends for sticking with you through the year and those shopkeepers for always lending you a ToonTask when you need one! The top three winners will be received a brand new in-game shirt depending on the category you placed in as well as a bunch of other goodies! The contest ends on December 7th at 11:59 PM Toontown Time (PST). So what are you waiting for? Get out there and be thankful!

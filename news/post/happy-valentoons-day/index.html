Happy ValenToon's Day!

Posted by: Your Secret Admirer on February 13, 2019 at 8:43 PM

It's the second most wonderful time of the year! I remember when I was still at the Toontown School House and just stared at your luscious clothing for hours on end. I put heart-shaped Laff boosters in your closet and peeked behind the corner giggling with my friends waiting for you to come and see them. 


Years later, you work on the shop down the other street. I ask Toons to deliver you love letters after the Cogs tried to steal our love away. You have such a way with words. Ruff, woof. For those of you without that special Toon in your life, fill your crush's day with love letters and just watch them pretend they don't know who you even are. Happy ValenToon's Day, lovelies.

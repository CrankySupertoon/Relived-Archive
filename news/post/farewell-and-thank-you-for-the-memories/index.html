Farewell and Thank You for the Memories!

Posted by: The Toontown Relived Team on June 13, 2020 at 11:38 PM

Well Toons, I can’t believe this is happening. I moved to this wonderful world just a few years ago and I’m for once, at a loss for words. I can’t even start to think about all the memories and magic that we’ve shared together. It feels like a lifetime has passed in these years and I want to thank all of the amazing Toons I’ve met along the way, you are the light that shines in the darkness. No matter what happens, know I will always be with you. Now and always, I love all of you. Thank YOU for making magic with me and keeping the light of Toontown shining bright. With that being said, it is with a heavy heart that today we announce that ToonTown Relived will be closing permanently on June 17, 2020 at 3:00 Toontown Time (Pacific Standard Time). Once this happens, all information stored in our database, database backups, and things alike will be completely erased.

There are many mysteries that evolved over the years, such as the disappearance of Doctor Surlee and Doctor Dimm, The Broken Road, The Monster, Ruffle’s Magical Powers, and how to finally save Toontown from the evil Cogs. While we may not have all the answers, we believe with a little creativity and magic you can find the answers you seek. We also think this may not be the end of these mysteries. There’s more than meets the eye and we may hear from these friends again someday. We will continue to send the Toon Resistance to search for our missing friends and maybe someday they will finally drive out the evil Cogs. Do not fear, we will not forget our friends just as we will never forget all of you out there.

Toons, this has been one heck of a ride. We’ve seen so much together over the years and this wouldn’t have been as special if it wasn’t for all of you. Our players mean so much to us, and to me--you are more than players. You are my friends. We care about you and we cannot express how much your support and loyalty has meant over the years. Please stay in touch and let us know if we can assist you in any way as we move on from Toontown Relived. We love you all, thank you for making our lives a lot more exciting. We hope you’ve made some magical memories to last a lifetime. Thank you for making our family part of yours. We will never forget you all. <3

You ARE Toon Enough.

The Start to a Brand New Year

Posted by: Party Planner Penelope on January 1, 2019 at 12:00 AM

See you next year, Toons. Heh, that joke always cracks me up. You know every year I've been telling myself the same thing over and over again, but for real this time, this year will be my year! I can just feel the strong motivation among the banging and booming going around that I'll be able to live up to my New Year's resolutions!

The fireworks are blasting away while 2018 sets. You know, it wasn't THAT bad of a year. We don't want you to miss a single part of this new year. I can guarantee you that this year will be bigger than any years before! Here's to a brand new and exciting year of Toontown Relived! Happy New Year!

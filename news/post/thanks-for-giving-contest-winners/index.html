"Thanks-For-Giving" Contest Winners!

Posted by: The Toontown Relived Team on December 8, 2019 at 12:00 AM

Seven awesome Toons have placed in the "Thanks-For-Giving" contest that we announced last week! The winners for the "Most Cog Buildings Defeated" category are Sir Adam CrunchyKits: 38, Lily: 27, Jake Cheesey Marble: 11, and Super Jellyroll BumbleSpeed: 11. Congratulations!

The next category was "Most Gifts Given" and we had three very generous winners! The winners are: Hayden: 102, Sweetie Piecakes: 74, and Frosty Night: 49. These Toons are considered some of the most generous Toons in all of Toontown Relived!

The winners will be receiving their items sometime within the next few days. Once again, great job everyone! You are definitely Toon Enough!
